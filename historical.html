LEBANON'S VAST HISTORY
From ancient Phoenician civilization to modern-day struggles for stability, Lebanon's story is one of a nation shaped by countless influences.
Its strategic location on the Mediterranean coast has made it a coveted prize for empires throughout history, leaving behind layers of cultural heritage and architectural marvels.
