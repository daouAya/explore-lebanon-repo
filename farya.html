Farya ski resort

   Farya ski resort:
    Where Adventure Meets Serenity

Nestled amidst Lebanon's rugged mountains, Faraya stands as a haven of natural beauty and tranquility. With its snow-capped peaks in winter and lush green landscapes in summer, it offers a breathtaking backdrop for outdoor adventures year-round.
But Faraya isn't just a haven for snow enthusiasts. In the warmer seasons, the village comes alive with vibrant colors and lush greenery, inviting visitors to explore its scenic trails and hidden treasures. From leisurely hikes to adrenaline-pumping adventures, there's something for everyone to enjoy amidst Faraya's natural wonders.

Did You Know?


Faraya, nestled amidst Lebanon's mountains, offers a delightful blend of winter sports, scenic hiking trails, and rich cultural heritage.
Whether you're carving through snow-covered slopes or immersing yourself in the charm of local villages, Faraya promises an unforgettable escape for all who visit




   Attractions and Activities:

Faraya, nestled in Lebanon's mountains, offers year-round attractions. Winter invites skiing at resorts, while hiking trails open in summer.
Beyond skiing and hiking, Faraya offers a range of outdoor activities, including mountain biking, rock climbing, and paragliding.
Adrenaline junkies will find plenty of opportunities to get their heart racing amidst the stunning natural scenery.
